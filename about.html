About Us

Kraken Woodwurks was created by owner and operator Kevin Hughes. Kevin has been building furniture for over 30 years. He has a passion for making something out of nothing. In light of this passion, he decided to turn his hobby into a business in 2014 and brought on board fellow craftspeople to further his mission to provide world-class quality furniture to those who are looking for something that is as much a peice of art as it is a peice of furniture.
